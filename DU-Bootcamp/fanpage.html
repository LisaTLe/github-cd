Biography

BTS, also known as Bangtan Boys, first debuted in June of 2013. The seven-member boy band works under the HYBE Music label, which was previouslty known as BigHit Music. About a year ago, HYBE bought over a big U.S. company that is known for managing some A-list celebrities like Justin Beiber, Ariana Grande, and even Demi Lovato.
